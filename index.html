About

We offer high quality English language courses to those living in the area, and to short-term visitors.

In terms of qualifications and experience as the Director of Studies I have a BA in English and History and MLitt in English from Trinity College Dublin, and Celta (Cambridge qualification for teaching English). I have been teaching English to foreign students for more than 20 years (and still love it!).

My experience is with teaching adults, and juniors, business and exam English, groups and one-to-ones and also training teachers of English both native and non-native speakers.

For those who live in the area I can provide dedicated exam preparation courses for FCE, CAE, IELTS or CPE!

For holiday courses... we enjoy welcoming students to improve their English, and for our visitors to discover our culture while we learn about theirs too.

Outside of classes, all activities are conducted through English. It's also your holiday, so you choose the type of activities you want to enjoy. Whether you're a golfer, a theatre buff, or into traditional Irish dancing (or all of these!) we can organise a specialised programme of activities to suit you.

The idea is for you to learn as much English as possible while at the same time having a memorable, fun-filled holiday! Susan
